What about food, clothing, and other equipment?
+

Food: Children must bring two snacks (morning and afternoon) and a nutritious packed lunch plus a water bottle.

Clothing: Dress for the weather and expect painting and outdoor activities ‚Äî comfortable walking shoes are recommended.

Other equipment: Unless required by facilitators, electronic devices (phones, tablets, games, etc.) should not be brought. Expensive or treasured items should be left at home.

What is the pickup time?
+

Pickup from school at 16:00 for CAMPS and 16:30 for CARE.

Only children with recorded parent permission may leave by themselves ‚Äî inform us at copencamp@gmail.com once enrolled.

If a child needs to leave early during the day, a parent must notify copencamp@gmail.com in advance.

What is the standard of conduct?
+

Children are expected to maintain the highest standard of conduct. Profanity, physical violence, threats, or otherwise dangerous or disruptive behaviour are strictly prohibited. Staff instructions and programme rules must be followed.

CopenCamp reserves the right to withdraw any student whose behaviour is considered incompatible with the programme; no refund will be given in such cases. Parents agree to pay for damage caused by their child and to support positive conduct standards.

How is payment handled?
+

After registration you will receive an email with account details. Please ensure the email you provide is correct.

Enrollment is confirmed only when payment is received into the Learning Centre bank account. After payment, please email payment details to copencamp@gmail.com.

What is the mode of communication?
+
All CopenCamp activities and instruction are conducted in English. A basic level of English is a prerequisite for participation.

What are the refund rules?
+
If you cancel after payment, no refund will be given. If a session is cancelled by us due to undersubscription, you will receive a full refund.
